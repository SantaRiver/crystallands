About the game

Crystal Lands is a game on Wax blockchain, where you can collect resources, hunt, upgrade lands and sell items on the market. You arrive on lands where crystal fewer has begun. Together with the rest of the miners, you work hard to get precious crystals and earn a fortune. At the same time hunters who managed to get good equipment are combing out the winding paths of mines and forests in search of treasure. And suppose you work hard, one day you will become a happy owner of a land that will attract more and more adventurers.

  • WHERE DO I START?

    You need mining tools or hunting weapons to start the game. They can be obtained by buying NFT on AtomicHub and activating it or crafted with in-game resources obtained in the market, and then you can go to any land to start working

  • HOW CAN I EARN?

    You can earn by hunting monsters, trading resources on the in-game marketplace or selling crafted tools and weapons on AtomicHub. Also, you can own a land and earn royalty from it

  • WHAT IS THE CRY TOKEN FOR?

    Token is used for crafting and buying in-game items

Health is a parameter used for hunting Monsters. Player has 100 HP, which regenerates by 1 per hour if Satiety is >70% (210). Cannot go below 1. This parameter does not affect the speed of work in buildings, even if low. When using equipment, HP consumption is reduced. HP does not decrease if your Satiety is low. Hunting without equipment requires a minimum of 11 HP.

Satiety is a parameter required for HP regeneration. Player has a maximum of 300 Satiety points, decaying at a rate of 1 per hour. To restore Satiety, your character needs to eat. This parameter does not affect the speed of work in buildings, even if low.

Energy is a Player’s Parameter required for most actions in the game. Player has a maximum of 100 units of Energy, regenerated 1 per hour. Also, Energy can be restored with food.

When buying NFT Land on AtomicHub, Player becomes its Owner. Owner receives a set % (customizable) from any activity of other players on their Land in CRY tokens, equivalent to the cost of resources on the market. Starter buildings on each Land are Forest, Mine and Market. The rest of the buildings need to be constructed. After a new building is constructed, new activities appear and the limit of Players who can work and hunt in Mine and Forest increases. When buying NFT Land from another Owner, all constructed buildings remain on it. Each land is assigned its own ID, by which Owner can invite other Players. All Lands are displayed in the general list, Lands with the fewest active Players appear at the top.

Forest - Player can work in Forest to obtain Wood and has a chance to get Apple Seeds.

When working on someone else’s Land, Player pays Owner a commission fee from the obtained resources. In Forest and Mine there is a limit of simultaneously working Players; Players who enter the Land after the Limit was reached receive a penalty for obtaining resources. Work consumes Energy which regenerates over time.

All actions in the game are performed on Land. When entering the game, Player chooses the Land on which he wants to work. When working or hunting on someone else’s land, Player pays Owner a commission fee from the obtained resources. On each Land there is a limit of Players for work and hunting; when it is exceeded, the percentage of obtained resources is reduced.

Workshop - in the workshop, Player can create the tools necessary for working in Mine and Forest - Picks and Axes.

Farm - food is grown on Farm. The player plants a Seed and visits the Farm every 3 hours to water it and continue the growth progress. After the Apple has ripened, the player can harvest it.

Foundry- in Foundry, Iron Ore is smelted into Iron Ingots using Wood.

Corral - Cows are kept in Сorral. Player starts growing a Cow and visits Corral every 3 hours to feed it and continue the growth progress. After raising a Cow, Player can get Meat and Leather.

Forge - equipment for hunting - weapons and armor - is created in Forge.
